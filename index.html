The problem we're fixing

Influencer marketing
is broken for everyone

Brands spend thousands on posts that flop. Creators spend hours negotiating deals that go nowhere. HypeNex fixes both sides at once.

For brands

You're paying for promises, not results

Traditional influencer marketing charges per post or per follower. A creator with 100k followers might get 200 views on your product. You've already paid. There's no refund.

For creators

You're undervalued unless you're already famous

Without a huge following, brands won't talk to you. You spend more time pitching than creating. When you do land a deal, negotiating fair pay is exhausting.

The old model

Agencies take 30–50%. ROI is unmeasured.

You have no idea if your £5,000 campaign generated £500 or £50,000 in value. Performance is never measured. Accountability doesn't exist.

For micro-creators

Great content. No clear path to income.

You make content people love. But without a massive following, there's no way to monetise it. TikTok's creator fund barely covers coffee. Brand deals feel out of reach.
